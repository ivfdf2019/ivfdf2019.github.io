Welcome

The Inter-Varsity Folk Dance Festival is coming to Edinburgh in 2019! Come join us from the 1st-3rd March 2019 for a weekend of music and dancing. For the most regular updates, don't forget to like the IVFDF 2019 Facebook page!

We have tickets available for the individual evening events (£10/£8), for the workshop and day events, or for the full weekend (£35/£20). We are proud to be the most affordable large folk dance festival in the UK!
