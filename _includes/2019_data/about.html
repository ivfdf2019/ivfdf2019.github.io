About

  • IVFDF

    The Inter-Varsity Folk Dance Festival, or IVFDF is the UKs longest running folk festival. Catering to between 600 - 900 people, the festival is run entirely by volunteers and is hosted by a different university each year spanning from Aberdeen in the north to Exeter in the south.
