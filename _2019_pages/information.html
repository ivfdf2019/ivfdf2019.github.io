Information will be available shortly!

Reception

The reception will be located in the Dome in the Potterow building. To get there, enter through the main doorway from Bristo Square and go straight through, we’ll be sat right there! The reception will be open from 18:00-23:00 on Friday and throughout the day on Saturday and Sunday morning. Please collect all weekend passes and merchandise from the reception on arrival. If you are a steward, pleasealso make sure that you report to the reception when you arrive.

Stewards

All Stewards who are on shift will be extremely happy to help with any questions you may have throughout the day and will be instantly recognisable by their bright blue T-shirts.

All stewards please come to the reception in the Dome when you arrive

Reps Meeting

The representatives’ meeting will be happening on Saturday at 12:00 in Room 1 in the Southside Community Centre. Come represent your society and shape the future of IVFDF!

Alcohol

There are various bars in Teviot and a bar in Pleasance. The Loft bar in Teviot is open only for IVFDF attendees , all other EUSA bars will still be open as normal. Hobgoblin Stage is a BYOB venue but due to licencing laws alcohol is not allowed in any other venue. If you want to buy alcohol Scottish licensing laws only allow shops to sell alcohol between 10am and 10pm. Alcohol is also not allowed to be consumed in public places without a licence, ie. outside.

Morris Tour

The Morris Tour is scheduled to start at midday on the Saturday. Please assemble beforehand in Bristo Square.

Scratch Band

The Scratch Band plays for the survivors’ ceilidh on Sunday. Practice for this will be on Sunday 9:00 - 11:45 in the Ochil Room, Pleasance. Everybody is welcome!

Showers

Showers will be available at Crags Community Sports Centre from 22:00 - 9:00 both nights. The spaces are limited so please be prepared to queue and shower fast! There are also showers available at the CSE (next to the Pleasance) during the day, however these showers will cost £3 unless you are a CSE member.

Sleeping

The main sleeping venue is the Crags Community Sports Centre. This venue will be open from 22:00 - 9:00 on both nights and there are showers available at the venue. Within the venue there will be a dedicated sleeping space for all our lovely stewards as well. When arriving at the sleeping venue, please make sure that you are not blocking a fire exit or blocking anyone’s way to the fire exit. Please remember to be quiet as it the venue is an a residential area.

Storage

Storage will be available on Friday night in the Potterow venue until midnight. On Saturday there will be storage available at the sleeping venue. On Sunday … and then please take your bags to the Survivors’ Ceilidh where there will be space for you to store them there.

Wheelchair Access

Most of the venues are wheelchair accessible. Unfortunately, some of the workshops will not be as they are taking place in older buildings. Rooms that are not accessible are the Upper Hall and the Highland room, both in the Pleasance. If you are having trouble accessing any other room please speak to one of our superb stewards in a bright blue shirt.
