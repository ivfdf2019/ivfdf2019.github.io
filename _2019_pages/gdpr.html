This is the GDPR policy for all data collected and processed during the process of running IVFDF 2019.

  • ‘IVFDF 2019’ is the IVFDF 2019 committee, and volunteers working on behalf of the IVFDF 2019 committee
  • Only data necessary for running of IVFDF 2019 will be collected, including:
    • Registrations (including payments and refunds),
    • Housing/indoor camping
    • Workshop planning
    • Communication with participants
  • No data collected or processed by IVFDF 2019 will be shared with third parties
  • This information will be deleted no more than a year after IVFDF 2019
  • Data Subjects may request access, modification and deletion of their identifiable data at any time
  • Complaints and requests may be emailed to ivfdfedinburgh2019@gmail.com
